Overview

Screenshot Resizer by Justbox is a standalone macOS application that runs entirely on your device. We take your privacy seriously ‚Äî this app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

No Data Collection

The application processes your screenshots locally on your Mac. It does not transmit any files, metadata, or analytics to external servers. There are no ads, telemetry, or third-party SDKs.

Internet Access

Screenshot Resizer operates fully offline. Internet access is used only when you click a support or marketing link in your web browser. The app itself never uploads or downloads any data automatically.

Crash Reports

The app does not automatically send crash or usage reports. If you choose to contact us about an issue, you may manually share information such as macOS version or app logs for troubleshooting purposes.

Third-Party Services

None. Screenshot Resizer does not integrate any third-party analytics, payment, or advertising frameworks.

Data Retention

Because no data is collected, there is nothing stored or retained on our servers. All exported images remain solely on your local drive.
