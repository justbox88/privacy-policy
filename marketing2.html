About

ScreenshotResizerApp by Justbox is a lightweight macOS tool for developers and designers who need perfectly sized App Store screenshots.

Drag your screenshot folder into the app, select device formats, and export optimized PNG or JPEG files in seconds.

Features

  • Ready-made sizes for all App Store devices
  • Custom width & height support
  • No-Crop Mode for non-destructive resizing
  • PNG / JPEG export with transparency or color background
  • Optional lossless compression via pngquant
  • Progress indicator & log output

Privacy

All processing happens locally on your Mac. No uploads. No tracking. No ads.
