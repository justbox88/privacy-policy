Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does Screenshot Resizer upload my files?
A: No. All image processing happens locally on your Mac. Nothing is ever uploaded.

Q: The app says "No images found". What should I do?
A: Ensure the selected folder contains PNG or JPEG files, not subfolders. You can drag the folder directly into the app window.

Q: Can I set my own resolution?
A: Yes! Enable the Custom Size toggle and enter your preferred width and height.
